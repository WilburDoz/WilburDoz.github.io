I spent a long time planning this trip. Here's a document I made so that people could see where I would be walking each day of the trip, allowing them to come and join me! When making my plans I relied a lot on random blokes putting shit on the internet, so here's me paying forward the favour.

It was an 11 day walk from the sea at the north of Wales, to the sea at the South.
