Not-so-serious Things

Welcome, web surfer, to my little corner of the interwebs.

Perhaps you arrived here with curiosity about my professional life?

  • I'm doing a PhD at the Gatsby Unit, in London. I work with Tim Behrens on biological structure learning, and Peter Latham on neural networks.
  • I interned in Prof. Chelsea Finn's lab in Stanford, working on structured model-based RL.
  • I previously interned at OIST in Prof. Erik de Schutter's group. I worked on biologically-plausible hierarchical reinforcement learning.
  • I attended the 2020 Imbizo computational neuroscience summer school, one of the best months of my life!
  • I first met theoretical neuroscience working for Prof. Cengiz Pehlevan at Harvard. I worked on theoretical olfaction.
  • I used to do condensed matter physics. I worked on acoustic metamaterial mimics of condensed matter systems in Prof. Jenny Hoffman's lab.
  • I did my undergrad in physics at Cambridge.

If you like this kind of stuff may I point you to a collection of my research projects? Or my CV? Perhaps even my Google Scholar?
